BIOGRAPHY

Ikechukwu "Ike" Ogbo (born 5 August 1982) is a student of Harvard Extension. A Nigerian of Igbo descent, Ike is a career engineer. Ike came to America to pursue his educational and career goals in August 2008, leaving a great fortune back in Nigeria.

Early life and education

Ike grew up in a family of six. The son of a Chief and an entrepreneur. Ike spent most of his early years travelling on vacation with his family. Ike received a dual BE in Mechanical and Production Engineering from Nnamdi Azikiwe University in Nigeria in 2005 and a dual MS in Computer Engineering and Technology Management from University of Bridgeport in May, 2011. Currently, Ike is working on an MA in Liberal Arts in Extension Studies with a concentration in Information Technology from Harvard University. Even though, Ike is not a nerd, he is more focus than an average American. His academic excellence has gained him a lot of favor in his 4 years in America.

Career

Ike has worked in different engineering fields. He went for a six month internship in a car assembly plant in Ningbo, China as an undergraduate Mechanical engineer. He finished his mandatory NYSC (National Youth Service Corp) program in the Power Holding Company of Nigeria in 2008. Ike worked as a graduate assistant in CTech IncUBator. While in University of Bridgeport, he put himself through school by exporting cars to Nigeria. Currently, he works as a support associate in Hedge Fund since August 2011.

Personal

Ike lives a healthy lifestyle. He never jokes with his workout regime and meal plan. Ike strongly believes in Whatever will be will be
